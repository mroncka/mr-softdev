## Content Pillars

1. **Build in Public** — real progress, real failures, real decisions
2. **AI × Dev** — autonomous pipelines, agents, ComfyUI, LLMs in practice
3. **Project Demos** — finished things with clear hook + impact
4. **Dev Commentary** — opinions on tools, AI models, workflows
5. **Fun Experiments** — pizza CV, finance bots, anime generators

---

## Hook Formulas

- "I built X that does Y" (demo)
- "My AI ran X without me" (agent log)
- "I automated X. Here's how." (tutorial adjacent)
- "Nobody talks about X" (opinion)
- "[Before/After]" (transformation)
